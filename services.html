Rock Fish and Salmon

Our Combo Rock Fish and Salmon package gives you the most bang for your buck. Imagine coming home with a limit of fresh salmon and rock fish! There could be well over 40lbs of filleted meat in your bag!

Salmon

People travel all from all over the globe to fish our waters for salmon. Come see why! Nothing is as comparable to landing a salmon (other than, perhaps putting it on the BBQ).

Rock Fish

We do something slightly different here on the Samurai. At Hooked On Mendo, we believe that part of Sport Fishing should include, well, Sport. Therefore, we use rods and reels that let the rock fish fight. Instead of using 40lbs line for a 3-`0 lbs fish, we use something that makes it much more exciting. If you've never tried it out, it's well worth spending a half day out on the beautiful Pacific.

Dungeoness Crab

Although we may not be the Deadliest Catch, our crab are just as tasty. Come see how it's all done and take a mess of crab home for dinner! You may even be able to spot whales, which tend to migrate during the same season as crab.
