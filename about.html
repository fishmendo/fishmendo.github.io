The Samurai

Fishing is in our BLOOD! We love the rugged Mendocino coast, and after a day on the Pacific with us, you'll understand why. We have some of the best fishing, in one of the most beautifule locales along the rugged Northern California Coast.

The Samurai is a 32x13 foot Radovich hull powered by twin screw Catterpillar 3116 diesel engines with a 6 KW northern lights Generator. 200 gallon live bait well. 1000 pound refrigerated fish hold, side scan sonar, furuno fish finder, Vhf radios, Radar and chart plotter. The cabin is very spacious and comfortable with lots of seating up in the fly bridge and in the cabin.

Our Team

Justin Wilder
Captain

Justin is the owner / operator of the Samurai . He grew up fishing off the coast of Mendocino, his uncle helped pioneer the charter boat industry in Fort Bragg many years ago, giving him lots of experience in the field. His aim is to bring his customers a new experience in the charter boat world, matching boat with an awesome crew to ensure you have a wonderful trip aboard the Samurai and will be one you won't forget. Captain Justin wants to bring you to the fish, and get fish on board for you, he can't wait to use the "welcome aboard stick"!

Kirk Naulty
Deckhand

Kirk has lived in Fort Bragg most of his life. He knows the coast inside and out. When he's not working on the boat (topside) he's probably spearfishing or abalone diving down below. His love for the ocean is only rivaled by passion to make sure everyone leaves with a full catch in their bags and smiles on their faces.
